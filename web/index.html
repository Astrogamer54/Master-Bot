Commands

  1. Music
  2. Command Description Usage
  3. /play Play any song or playlist from youtube, you can do it by searching for a song by name or song url or playlist url !play darude sandstorm
  4. /pause Pause the current playing song !pause
  5. /resume Resume the current paused song !resume
  6. /leave Leaves voice channel if in one !leave
  7. /remove Remove a specific song from queue by its number in queue !remove 4
  8. /queue Display the song queue !queue
  9. /shuffle Shuffle the song queue !shuffle
  10. /skip Skip the current playing song !skip
  11. /skipall Skip all songs in queue !skipall
  12. /skipto Skip to a specific song in the queue, provide the song number as an argument !skipto 5
  13. /volume Adjust song volume !volume 80
  14. /music-trivia Engage in a music trivia with your friends.
  15. /loop Loop the currently playing song or queue !loop
  16. /lyrics Get lyrics of any song or the lyrics of the currently playing song !lyrics song-name
  17. /now-playing Display the current playing song with a playback bar !now-playing
  18. /move Move song to a desired position in queue !move 8 1
  19. /queue-history Display the queue history !queue-history
